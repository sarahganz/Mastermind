MASTERMIND INSTRUCTIONS
V color + V place
V color + X place
X color + X place

Goal of the game:
Figure out what the random combination of four colors is before the timer runs out.

Start by choosing a combination of four colours.
You can use the same color more than once.
Then you will get hints circled around the four colours you chose.

Black = right color & right place
White = right color & wrong place

HAVE FUN!
